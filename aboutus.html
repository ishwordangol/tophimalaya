About Us

Who we are

Top Himalaya Guides stands out due to its distinctive ownership and operation by Nepalese IFMGA certified international mountain guides. This ownership ensures a profound understanding of the Himalayan terrain and culture, as well as a commitment to the highest standards of safety and expertise. The mountains aren't just a workplace but a cherished home for the guides, who take immense pleasure in sharing their intimate knowledge and experiences with climbers and trekkers from all corners of the globe. This genuine connection to the mountains fosters a welcoming and inclusive atmosphere, where adventurers of all backgrounds can come together to explore and appreciate the wonders of the Himalayas.

Your guide

Tshering Pande Bhote

Company director is IFMGA/NNMGA Certified Mountain Guide (Instructor).Long line Helicopter Rescue Specialist Tshering Pande Bhote has already been an accomplished Top Himalaya Guides, having guided clients Seven times to the top of Mt. Everest, before making several trips to New Zealand and France to become one of the first Nepalese to gain the international IFMGA certified mountain guide qualification. In the process he excelled in all areas of technical climbing, putting up a new route on the south face of Mt Cook, making the first ascent (in winter) of the east face headwall of Haidinger, and soloing the classic steep south face of Douglas Peak (also in winter).
