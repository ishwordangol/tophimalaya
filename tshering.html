About Tshering Pande Bhote

About me

Long line Helicopter Rescue Specialist Tshering Pande Bhote has already been an accomplished Top Himalaya Guides, having guided clients Seven times to the top of Mt. Everest, before making several trips to New Zealand and France to become one of the first Nepalese to gain the international IFMGA certified mountain guide qualification. In the process he excelled in all areas of technical climbing, putting up a new route on the south face of Mt Cook, making the first ascent (in winter) of the east face headwall of Haidinger, and soloing the classic steep south face of Douglas Peak (also in winter).
